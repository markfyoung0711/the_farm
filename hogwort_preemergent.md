# Hogwort (Goatweed) - Pre-Emergent Treatment

Pre-emergent herbicides prevent hogwort (silverleaf/common goatweed) seeds from germinating. Apply before green-up for best results.

## Recommended Products

- **Milestone**
- **GrazonNext HL**
- Dedicated pre-emergent herbicide products

## Important Notes

- Pre-emergents do **not** knock back existing plants — they only prevent new seedlings from emerging.
- Plan follow-up applications as seeds can continue to germinate throughout the season.
- Even with pre-emergent treatment, post-emergent follow-up may be needed for established plants.
