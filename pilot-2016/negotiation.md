## Golden Rules

- **Never reveal your $8,000 floor** until you're ready to close
- **Lead with the Carfax and service history** every time you counter
- **"Already priced in"** shuts down buyers trying to double-dip on repairs
- **Calm beats eager** — never sound relieved or desperate
- **Silence after a counter** — whoever talks next usually blinks first
- **Cash only** — no Venmo, Zelle, cashier's checks, or third parties
- **Meet in public** — gas station, police station parking lot, or bank
- **Photo the license** before any test drive
- **File a Vehicle Transfer Notification** at txdmv.gov after the sale
